Visit Garowe


The town was known as “Garowin”
during the Middle Ages, and was
the site of a major battle between
the Majeerteen Sultanate and the
Portuguese in 1591.
It was subsequently captured by the
Ottoman Empire, and became the
capital of the Puntland region.

Jowhar has a population of about
